## Core Philosophy (still valid)

- Every tap or input should be reducible to a yes/no/modify prompt where possible
- Log food in under 5 seconds — the entire product lives or dies on this
- The app should treat logging as **behavior support**, not data entry
- Most people eat the same 5–10 meals repeatedly — build for that, not for novelty
- Speed, search quality, habit automation matter more than fancy AI features

## Ideas Deferred to Post-Launch

These require real users before they're worth building:

- Community food voting / confidence scores / verified food promotion
- AI photo calorie detection
- Natural language food entry ("2 slices of bread with ham")
- Grocery list generation from frequent foods
- Advanced macro coaching (cutting/bulking/maintenance presets)
- Weight trend prediction
- Premium tier + pricing (29–59 SEK/month or 299–399 SEK/year suggested)
- Swedish/Nordic market focus → expand to EU later
